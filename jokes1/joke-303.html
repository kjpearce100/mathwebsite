GASTROINTESTINAL TRACT

There a striking and compelling analogy between the progression of nutrients through a man's gastrointestinal tract and the phases and circumstances of a man's life.

Indeed, for a young man there is nothing more satisfying than a good kiss. For middle-aged man in the prime of his life there is nothing more satisfying than a good meal. And, for an old man there is nothing more satisfying than good bowel movement.
