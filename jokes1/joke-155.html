DINOSAURS

One day 3 dinosaurs named "Foot", "Foot-Foot", and "Foot-Foot-Foot" were out playing .

Suddenly, Foot turned to Foot-Foot and said, "I don't feel very well!"

Later, Foot got sick and died.

One day, "Foot-Foot" and "Foot-Foot-Foot" were out playing.

Suddenly, Foot-Foot turned to Foot-Foot-Foot and said, "I don't feel very well!"

And Foot-Foot-Foot said, "Well you'd better get well pretty soon since we already have one Foot in the Grave!"
