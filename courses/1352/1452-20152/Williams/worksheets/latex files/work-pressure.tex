\question Clueless Morgan has trapped Kermit the Frog in a small 
concrete room.  The room has one window which is 3 ft wide and 2 ft high.
The top of the window touches the ceiling.  Kermit realizes his only hope
of escape is to activate the sprinkler and completely flood the room.
\begin{parts}
\part When the room is completely filled with water, what will be the force on
the window?
\vspace{3in}
\part If the window can withstand 350 lb of force without breaking, 
will Kermit's plan allow him to escape or will he be left paddling for dear
life?
\vspace{.5in}
\end{parts}

\question A gate is built into the side a dam.
Set up an integral to determine the 
 force on the gate if it were in the shape of the upper half of disc of
radius 4 (and the top of the gate is 2 ft below the surface of the water)?
