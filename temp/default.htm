Family
Meet our family. We reside in Lubbock, Texas and have been in Lubbock since 1980, when we moved here from graduate school at SUNY at Albany. Before pursuing graduate work at Albany, we (Judy and Kent) were both at Brigham Young University (separately and singlely) – Judy worked then in the admissions office and Kent went to school. Originally, Judy is from western Minnesota and Kent is from Utah.

Currently, Kent is the (Interim) Chair of the Department of Mathematics and Statistics at Texas Tech University. His professional website contains links to his vitae, research interests and teaching resources. Judy, following quilting interests, works part-time at two local quilt stores.

We have five active children, ages 24 through 12.

  • Kip is a senior, enrolled at Texas Tech University, majoring in Personal Financial Planning.
  • Micah is a junior at Texas Tech University, majoring in Mathematics. Micah spent two years in the California San Fernando Mission speaking and teaching in Korean.
  • Kali is a junior, enrolled at Brigham Young University, majoring in Environmental Science.
  • Quinn is a junior at Coronado High School and is a member of Coronado's UIL Math Team.
  • McKenzie is enrolled Smylie Wilson Middle School and is enrolled in CA.

Our family interests in revolve around church, family, raising irises, quilting and extended family (in Minnesota and Utah).
